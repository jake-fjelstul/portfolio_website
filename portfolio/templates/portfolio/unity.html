Kingdom Ruins

A virtual recreation of my board game Kingdom Ruins, brought to life as an interactive digital experience. Kingdom Ruins is a strategic tabletop experience where players navigate the crumbling remnants of a once-great civilization, balancing the desperate need for resource management with the looming threat of total collapse. You aren't just building a kingdom; you're trying to sustain one against the encroaching decay, forcing you to make tough tactical calls on whether to reinforce your defenses or scavenge for lost relics. The gameplay feels both urgent and methodical, capturing that specific tension of trying to forge a legacy in a world that is actively trying to slide into history.

Tiny World

Don't crash!

Tiny World is a 3D arcade driving game developed in Unity (C#) that utilizes custom spherical physics to create a "small planet" gameplay loop. The project features a specialized player controller designed to handle vehicle movement and gravity on a continuously rotating, non-planar surface. A key technical component is the dynamic environmental progression system: as the player navigates the procedurally generated course avoiding obstacles, the game logic manages real-time asset substitution to seamlessly evolve the world's aesthetic and difficulty from a historic era into a futuristic cityscape based on survival duration.
